At A Glance... LJ Innovation Village

LJ Innovation Village is a platform to showcase innovative projects, products, start-ups by innovators. It provides anopportunity to innovators to interact with students, faculties, industry people and investors.

Innovation involves deliberate application of information, imagination and initiative in deriving greater or different values from resources, and includes all processes by which new ideas are generated and converted into useful products.Innovation & Invention are processes that look at extraordinary, new things, events and paths for the betterment of humanity. When students become creative that they will become innovators and entrepreneurs and then the innovation ecosystem can develop.


To nurture these skills of students (youth), LJ Group of Institutes organizes LJ Innovation Village every year in January - February. It brings out hidden creativity of students, improves their skills and enhancing performance by focusing on overall innovative development.


LJ Innovation Village is being organized annually since 2013, more than 700 projects have been showcased by over 3000 students of Degree/Diploma Engineering, Pharmacy and MCA. More than three thousands visitors including students, professors, industry people and investors visited the event every year.


Students visit some places and look into the social problems, then find out solutions by their innovative ideas and make projects. All these projects mentored and evaluated by various Industry People. After the evaluation process, best projects have been selected for next stage (i.e. Start-up).


In 2016, Start-up week has been launched and best projected were showcased. During this process, few projects have been selected for product and funded by LJ Group of Institutes. Till now more than 25 Projects have been commercialized as Start-up.
